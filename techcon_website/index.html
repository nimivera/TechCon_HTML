Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small meetup of developers and innovators. Over the years, it has evolved into a global platform for showcasing technology, sharing ideas, and building communities.

Our Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to drive innovation through collaboration, education, and inclusivity. We aim to empower developers, engineers, and creators to build a better tech future.

Past Speakers

Ada Lovelace

Ada Lovelace is known as the first computer programmer. Her theoretical work laid the groundwork for future generations of coders and technologists.

Alan Turing

Alan Turing's contributions to cryptography and artificial intelligence remain vital to modern computing. He spoke at TechCon 2015 on machine learning's theoretical foundations.

Grace Hopper

Known for inventing the compiler, Grace Hopper inspired generations with her vision of user-friendly programming languages. She headlined TechCon 2018.
